RANDOM ROGUE

a game of poor decisions

A lofi roguelike where every world simulates 250 years of history before you arrive — then makes fun of you for dying in it.

▶ PLAY IN BROWSER SOURCE

The world doesn't wait for you

Before your first step, the recipe runs: geography, factions, wars, artifacts, named beasts, plagues, and the occasional thing that falls screaming from the sky. Cities burn and become the dungeons you'll loot. Then you're born into the wreckage with six stats, a rusty sword, and no expectations.

Every card is a choice

2–4 options, visible dice rolls, outcomes that are tragic, funny, or ironically good. Usually all three, eventually.

Every rumor is real

Tavern gossip, weathered books, and treasure maps quote the world's actual chronicle. 15% of rumors are embellished. Good luck.

Every item is tied to something

A buckler stamped with the name of a city that burned in year 141. A spyglass carved with a dead man's IOU. Blessing, curse, or just history — the game will never tell you which.

Every seed is a world

Same seed, same world, same history — on any machine. Find something ridiculous? Send a friend the number.
