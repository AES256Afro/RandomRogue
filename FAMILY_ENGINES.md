# Family Engine Map

Status: working document for the Release 20 narrative restructure.

Random Rogue's connected families are a comedy anthology. Each family is a
funny story with recurring characters, a specific joke, and an ending worth
retelling in a tavern. The politics of the world stays where it belongs: in
the walls. Rent exists, forms exist, wages exist, the System files everything.
Nobody delivers a thesis about any of it. If a card would survive with its
jokes removed and its message intact, the card is wrong.

The Release 18 wave gave all 59 families the same story shape: a collective
petitions, power retaliates, a ledger opens. This map replaces that at the
architecture level. Every family gets its own comedic engine, its own story
shape, its own small cast, and its own payoff, decided before any prose is
written, so no two families can converge no matter who writes them.

## Rules that bind every rewrite

1. Humor and irony first. Narrative second. Message a distant third, and
   only ever by allusion. The player should finish a family remembering the
   characters and the jokes, not the cause.
2. Stories are about people, not movements. Each family gets a small
   recurring cast, two to four named oddballs whose arcs carry the beats.
   Collective nouns (leagues, circles, assemblies) are background scenery,
   mentioned the way weather is mentioned.
3. Organizing vocabulary is rationed. Words like solidarity, comrade,
   organize, and collective belong to at most a handful of outcomes across
   the whole deck. Everywhere else the same idea arrives dressed as a
   favor, a rota, a promise, a debt between friends, or a shared umbrella.
4. The family's specific joke gets paid in the outcomes, not just announced
   in the setup. If a family owns a canary with a management title, the
   canary appears in result text and has an arc.
5. Choices escalate beat to beat. No choice text repeats across beats. An
   option the player took earlier may return changed, curdled, or as a
   punchline the world has already refuted.
6. One ironic mechanism per family inverts by the final beat: the tool of
   power ends up doing something humane, or the heroes' own tool gets
   bureaucratized against them. Alternate the direction.
7. Not every family resolves by anyone winning anything. Shapes include
   audits, heists, trials, seances, cook-offs, wakes, courtships, reunions,
   repair jobs, and booking errors. Endings may land on a joke, an image,
   a person, or a quiet ambiguity. A changed institution is one possible
   ending flavor, used sparingly.
8. Failure branches are punchlines with consequences, never grimdark.
   Death is a filing event; the paperwork for it is extensive.
9. Greed and cowardice options are genuinely tempting and funny, not
   narrator-punished. Sometimes they simply work.
10. Victims are never the joke. In the heavy families (archive_names,
    border_census, open_prison) the comedy aims exclusively at the
    machinery, and sparingly.
11. No em dashes, no en dashes, anywhere, ever.
12. Mechanical scaffolding (effects, scheduling, stage flags, ids, quotas)
    is preserved byte for byte. Only prose, choice labels, and fingerprints
    move.

## The engines

Payoff lines below are the joke the family must land, not a moral.

### Legalese as horror: the fine print is the monster

- funeral_insurance: claims-adjustment farce. Death is ruled a preexisting
  condition. The actuary attends his first actual funeral and files himself
  as a loss.
- debt_jubilee: accounting seance in the tavern. Bar tabs outlive their
  drinkers. The calculator absolves all debts the night somebody finally
  feeds the sandwich it demanded as collateral.
- moon_landlord: small-claims comedy on the moon. A tenant counter-invoices
  for supplying their own gravity by remaining heavy. The lease concedes.
- oxygen_patent: not an uprising, a product recall. Air fails to meet
  advertised specification. The sigh meter is called as a witness and
  hyperventilates.
- grain_futures: the harvest is fine; the paperwork causes the famine. The
  scarecrow goes long on actual grain and is promoted to oracle.
- universal_repair: episodic road story. Every stop is one broken thing,
  one impossible warranty, one stubborn tinker. It ends when the Saints'
  own reliquary breaks and the only repairman in range fixes it for free,
  out of warranty, on purpose, without a word.
- flood_housing: the landlord claims the tide. Rent is charged on rescue
  rafts until the tide is registered as a tenant with seniority and
  immediately violates the quiet hours.

### Cosmic farce: enormous scale meets petty administration

- alien_embassy: first-contact comedy. The ambassador communicates
  exclusively through soup; the Protocol Office's forms cannot capture
  flavor. The final treaty is a recipe with footnotes.
- asteroid_mine: survival farce. Repossessing spacesuits in vacuum has one
  flaw everyone but the agent can name. He discovers it from inside an
  airlock, and learns the precise market value of a second person.
- company_colony: founding-myth satire. The charter's map is of a different
  moon entirely. A town accidentally happens in the gap between the flag
  and the lunch.
- mountain_observatory: the comet arrives early, and for free, out of
  spite. Celestial bodies do not read terms of service.
- clone_personhood: tribunal farce. Every personhood test the company
  proposes, the original also fails. The mirror, called as a hostile
  witness, declines to take sides.
- robot_redundancy: hospice comedy for machines, melancholy permitted. The
  toaster's redundancy speech enters the Chronicle as literature.
- portal_timeshare: DONE. Booking-error farce; the invented sixth self gets
  a name, a wage, and opinions about receipts.

### Ceremonial nonsense: institutions defeated by their own rules

- royal_succession: the crown conducts job interviews at the constitutional
  picnic, and after interviewing everyone declines to fill the position.
- postponement_committee: filibuster judo, a comedy of agendas. The motion
  to define the word motion is amended until it moves.
- gentle_schism: doctrinal cook-off. Jurisdiction over the holy spoon is
  settled by who feeds strangers best. The spoon rules by serving.
- checkpoint_law: compliance comedy. Travelers obey the barrier so
  literally that it arrests itself.
- parade_route: warfare by city planning. The permit clerk routes the
  parade into a roundabout that has no exit clause.
- revolution_office: the Central Office of Spontaneity schedules the
  uprising in advance. Three neighbors miss it and fix a fountain instead,
  which turns out to be the only unsanctioned act of the year.
- free_kitchen: health-inspection farce. The midnight kitchen must be
  invisible and delicious simultaneously. The inspector's ladle defects
  first; the inspector follows his ladle.

### Scam and caper: nerve, verbal play, and pirate argument

- pirate_constitution: the ship's articles are drafted mid-voyage over the
  Admiral's gavel. The parrot's constitutional objections are all
  sustained.
- museum_return: a reverse heist. Break in to put everything back. The
  museum's own provenance files are the confession.
- memory_market: con-artist duel. A broker who short-sells embarrassment is
  sold a fabricated golden age, and ends up buying his own childhood back
  at a loss.
- strike_press: dueling newspapers printed on the same presses, day and
  night editions. The two papers review each other. Neither is neutral;
  only one admits it.
- ship_mutiny: the mutiny proceeds clause by clause through the ship's own
  articles while a storm files an amicus brief.
- refugee_ferry: crossing caper. The customs goose is won over by correct
  bread. Paperwork drowns; people do not.
- arms_factory: conversion caper. The night shift retools the line in
  secret, and the audit finds plowshares it cannot prove were ever bombs.

### Humane monsters: creatures as people with boundaries and workloads

- monster_local: workplace comedy from below. Tourists are the occupational
  hazard. The hydra spends seven beats achieving internal quorum, one head
  at a time.
- cryptid_consent: a courtship of consent. The last moon-winged witness
  says no, and the no holds. The naturalist's masterpiece is a page left
  deliberately blank.
- forest_commons: the trial of a tree for unlawful standing. Character
  witnesses span four generations, and the tree outlives the verdict.
- water_council: the river is ordered to present identification, and
  attends the hearing personally, through the doors, at flood stage.
- swamp_pipeline: the dead-frog safety mascot becomes beloved, therefore
  unkillable, therefore load-bearing.
- demon_clinic: DONE. Soup outranks committees; the form learns to say
  Other, please exist.
- haunted_warehouse: DONE. Death as HR process; the dead clock out at
  last.

### Melancholy interiors: wry, sad, jokes aimed strictly upward

- failed_commune: reunion drama. Old friends, old blame, and a chair that
  keeps electing itself. They hold one more meeting, correctly, at last,
  and it is mostly about who brings what.
- veterans_peace: recruiters mine mourning; the veterans throw an honest
  wake instead, with the stories the medals leave out. The medal meeting
  adjourns forever for lack of attendance.
- archive_names: quiet procedural. A censor allergic to alphabetical order
  is the only joke in the family; the names are never the punchline.
  Alphabetical order becomes a kind of prayer.
- open_prison: visiting-hours drama at the gate, one family at a time. The
  rack's adjustable guilt setting is discovered to be decorative.
- police_clock: the families read tomorrow's arrests off the station clock
  and quietly rearrange the neighborhood around them. The wig issues one
  final warrant, for the clock itself.
- mercenary_peace: job retraining for violence professionals. The sword's
  hostile unemployment claim is approved, setting precedent nobody is
  prepared for.

### System mockumentary: the org chart is the dungeon

- dock_ledger: the efficiency audit eats its author. The ceremonial
  clipboard splits open in public, and everyone learns what dignity was
  storing.
- healer_strike: hospital billing farce. The healers treat everyone free
  and bill the Temple for it. Tuesday is found medically necessary.
- school_sale: parents' evening. The fraudulent tuition invoice is assigned
  as homework, and the students show their work.
- union_election: a comedy of internal bureaucracy. The Permanent Interim
  Leadership loses to a quorum it certified itself, twice.
- coop_capture: boardroom body-snatchers. The mission statement slowly
  replaces everyone's vocabulary until one clerk reads the founding
  minutes aloud, in the original accent.
- ration_algorithm: debugging procedural. The fix is a code review; the
  comments name the author's boss.
- mine_rescue: rescue clock versus paperwork clock. The canary defects and
  leads the way down.
- ministry_denial: press-conference farce. The printers publish the weather
  and are charged with sedition. The podium files its own grievance.
- public_transit: the ghost timetable. Drivers and riders run the
  unprofitable minutes themselves, and the profitable minutes discover
  loneliness.
- public_library: archival mystery. The late fee older than the kingdom
  traces to the founding theft of the kingdom. The book is returned,
  anonymously, overnight.
- desalination: weather versus billing. The rain tariff meets a storm that
  refuses to invoice.
- tenant_tower: siege as variety show. The eviction trumpet, which only
  plays indoors, closes the tenants' victory concert, by request.
- seed_vault: heirloom court. Grandmothers with receipts. The turnip is
  acquitted on grounds of prior art.
- plague_market: bedside vigil dramedy. The medicine corner gets cornered
  right back. The vulture's waistcoat is donated, and worn.
- border_census: dry sabotage thriller, comedy rationed. The abacus that
  cannot count itself is finally asked to. The registry fails closed,
  forever.

## Production order

Batch 0 retunes the three finished sample families to this revision: fewer
committees, more named characters, organizing vocabulary cut to allusion.

Then batches of roughly ten families, one digest per batch for review before
the next begins. Suggested order: System mockumentary first (largest bucket,
closest to the tonal spine), then Legalese, Caper, Ceremonial, Cosmic,
Monsters, Melancholy last (hardest register to hold).

The seven r18 ending cards are rewritten by hand after the families, since
they read the accumulated world and deserve the best sentences in the deck.

After all batches: harden the automated gates so the opening-uniqueness check
reads the card body rather than the title line, and add a repeated-sentence
detector across the whole deck.
